Import workouts from Strava

Request your Strava export (Settings → My Account → Download or Delete Your Account), unzip it, and choose the activities.csv here. Each workout is added to your schedule on its date as an Exercise event.

Change password

Your entries stay readable — nothing is re-encrypted. Only the key that protects them is re-locked with the new password.

Recovery key

Your entries are encrypted in your browser, so a forgotten password can't be undone by resetting it — this key is the only way back in. Save it somewhere safe and private.

Download everything to a file you can keep somewhere safe, or restore from one. Importing replaces what's currently in the app.

Permanently deletes your schedule, journals, goals, to-dos, and rants. This can't be undone.
